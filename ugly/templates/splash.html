Ugly Reader

The ugliest Google Reader replacement on the market.

Ugly Reader provides an interface between your favorite blogs and the best Google Reader replacement yet: Gmail. All you have to do is tell us what feeds you want to read and we'll deliver them directly to your Gmail account. Don't worry, we won't clog your inbox; all of the posts will be sorted in nested labels.

To get started, all you have to do is login using your Gmail account and add some RSS feeds.
